Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ's

What should i wear?

Wear something comfortable that you can move freely in, unrestricting shorts or leggings are best

Do i need my own equipment?

When you first start, its okay to bring no equipment, but after a month of training we reccommend you get your own hand wraps, gloves, and shin gaurds

Do i need a mouthguard?

It is not essential but we do reccommend bringing a mouthguard once you've decided to stick with muay thai

I've never trained before, can i come to the mixed adult session

If you've never trained before we reccommend you attend the begginer course first
